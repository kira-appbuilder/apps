# WhisperSpace

A private micro-journaling app that creates a safe digital sanctuary for your thoughts. WhisperSpace uses local-only storage with gentle encryption to protect your privacy in an age of surveillance anxiety.

## Features

- **Three sentence limit** - Encourages concise, focused reflection
- **Local-only storage** - Your thoughts never leave your device
- **Gentle encryption** - Basic protection for your private entries
- **Auto-deletion** - Entries disappear after 30 days unless you choose to keep them
- **Beautiful typography** - Crimson Text and Source Sans create a calming writing experience
- **Dark, textured interface** - Easy on the eyes for late-night journaling

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Write your thoughts (maximum 3 sentences)
3. Click "Whisper" to save your entry
4. Entries automatically delete after 30 days unless you click "keep"
5. All data stays on your device - no internet required after loading

## Digital Privacy as Self-Care

WhisperSpace treats privacy as a form of self-care. Your thoughts are encrypted and stored locally, creating a truly private space for reflection without the anxiety of digital surveillance. The 30-day auto-deletion encourages letting go while the "keep" option preserves what matters most.
