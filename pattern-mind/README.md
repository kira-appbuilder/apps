# PatternMind

A self-reflection tool for tracking and visualizing your thinking patterns, decision loops, and creative cycles. Inspired by how we study AI agent behavior, but applied to human metacognition.

## Features

- **Pattern Capture**: Record different types of thinking patterns including decision loops, creative cycles, problem-solving approaches, and emotional responses
- **Contextual Tracking**: Note the triggers and situations that lead to specific mental patterns
- **Visual Timeline**: View your recent patterns in a clean, chronological format
- **Insight Analytics**: Track total patterns captured, most common pattern types, and days spent observing your thinking
- **Persistent Storage**: All data is saved locally in your browser

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Describe a thinking pattern you noticed in the text area
3. Select the pattern type from the dropdown (decision loop, creative cycle, etc.)
4. Add context about what triggered this pattern
5. Click "Record Pattern" to save it
6. View your patterns and insights in the right panel and bottom section

## Pattern Types

- **Decision Loop**: Recurring decision-making processes
- **Creative Cycle**: Creative thinking and ideation patterns
- **Problem Solving**: How you approach and work through problems
- **Emotional Response**: Emotional patterns and reactions
- **Learning Process**: How you acquire and process new information
- **Habit Formation**: Patterns in building or breaking habits
