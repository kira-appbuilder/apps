# Digital Garden Curator

A contemplative app for collecting and synthesizing scattered inspiration throughout the week. Like tending a garden of ideas, it provides gentle rituals for planting thoughts, nurturing connections, and harvesting insights.

## Features

- **Plant Seeds**: Capture articles, thoughts, quotes, images, and conversations with personal reflections
- **Garden Overview**: Track your collection with simple statistics and growth patterns
- **Weekend Ritual**: Discover unexpected connections between your collected ideas
- **Harvest Insights**: Export your garden as a structured reflection document

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Start by planting your first seed - add something that caught your attention recently
3. Throughout the week, continue adding inspirations with your personal reflections
4. On weekends, use the "weekend ritual" to discover patterns and connections
5. Export your insights when you're ready to harvest deeper understanding

## Design Philosophy

Inspired by the slow, seasonal rhythms of gardening, this app encourages mindful collection and reflection rather than rapid consumption. It's designed for depth over speed, connection over accumulation.

Your data is stored locally in your browser - your garden grows privately and remains under your control.
