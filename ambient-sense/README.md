# AmbientSense

A gentle environmental awareness companion that uses your device's sensors to create subtle notifications about air quality, light patterns, and sound levels throughout your day.

## Features

- **Light Pattern Monitoring**: Tracks ambient light levels and reflects natural rhythms
- **Sound Level Awareness**: Gentle monitoring of your acoustic environment without recording
- **Air Quality Simulation**: Provides awareness of breathing quality through subtle visual cues
- **Ambient View Mode**: Full-screen gradient overlay that paints soft colors reflecting your environment
- **Privacy-First**: All sensor data stays on your device - nothing is stored or transmitted
- **Bilingual Support**: Available in English and Japanese (日本語)

## How to Use

1. Open the app in a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
2. Click "Enable Sensors" to grant microphone and sensor permissions
3. The app will begin monitoring your environment with gentle, non-intrusive readings
4. Use "Ambient View" to see a full-screen visualization of your environment
5. Click anywhere on the ambient overlay to exit back to the main interface

## Sensor Fallbacks

If sensors aren't available or permissions are denied, the app gracefully falls back to realistic simulations that still provide the gentle awareness experience.

## Philosophy

Instead of aggressive alerts that demand attention, AmbientSense paints soft gradients and provides gentle awareness cues. It helps you notice the world around you by creating a subtle, ambient layer of environmental information that enhances rather than interrupts your daily rhythm.
