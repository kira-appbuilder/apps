# ConversationStarter

A simple webapp that provides contextual conversation prompts for different social situations like gyms, coffee shops, and co-working spaces. Features cultural sensitivity toggles for appropriate tone and context-awareness.

## Features

- **Three Social Contexts**: Gym, Coffee Shop, Co-working Space
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Toggle between formal/casual tone and cultural awareness
- **Bilingual Support**: English and Japanese interface
- **Contextual Prompts**: Situation-appropriate conversation starters
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Select your social situation (Gym, Coffee Shop, or Co-working)
3. Adjust cultural settings:
   - **Formal Tone**: Toggle for more polite, structured conversation starters
   - **Culture-Aware**: Enables culturally sensitive and contextually appropriate prompts
4. Click "Generate New Prompt" to get a conversation starter
5. Use the language toggle (top-right) to switch between English and Japanese

## Cultural Sensitivity Features

- **Formal vs Casual**: Adapts language formality level
- **Cultural Awareness**: Considers cultural context and appropriate social boundaries
- **Bilingual**: Authentic conversation starters in both English and Japanese
- **Context-Aware**: Different prompts for different social environments

The app is designed to help break the ice in social situations while being mindful of cultural differences and social contexts.
