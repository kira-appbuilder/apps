# PaperBreath

A gentle bureaucracy companion that transforms overwhelming paperwork and administrative tasks into manageable breathing rhythms.

## What it does

PaperBreath helps you break down complex bureaucratic tasks (taxes, permits, insurance claims, license renewals, etc.) into calm, manageable steps. Instead of anxiety-inducing todo lists, it creates a gentle visual flow that guides you through each phase with optional breathing exercises between sections.

## Key Features

- **Task Breakdown**: Describe any overwhelming paperwork and get a structured, step-by-step approach
- **Breathing Exercises**: Optional guided breathing sessions between steps to maintain calm
- **Gentle Flow**: Soft, calming visual design that reduces bureaucratic anxiety
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual indicators showing your journey through complex processes
- **Bilingual Support**: Available in English and Japanese (日本語)
- **Self-Contained**: Works immediately when opened in any web browser

## How to use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Describe your bureaucratic task or paperwork challenge
3. Click "Break Into Gentle Steps" to get a manageable breakdown
4. Follow each step at your own pace
5. Use breathing breaks whenever you feel overwhelmed
6. Complete each step and move forward with confidence

## Examples of tasks it helps with

- Filing taxes for freelance income
- Applying for business permits
- Organizing medical insurance claims  
- Renewing professional licenses
- Completing government forms
- Processing legal documentation

PaperBreath transforms institutional chaos into human-centered, breathable moments.
