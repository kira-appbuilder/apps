# Ikigai Compass

A gentle daily reflection app that helps users navigate their purpose through the four elements of Ikigai.

## What is Ikigai?

Ikigai is a Japanese concept meaning "reason for being" - the intersection of what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, and what sustains you.

## Features

- **Daily Reflections**: Answer four simple questions each day
- **Visual Compass**: See your balance across the four Ikigai elements
- **Journey History**: Track your reflections over time
- **Beautiful Design**: Modern dark theme with smooth animations
- **Local Storage**: Your reflections are saved privately in your browser

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Reflect on the four questions:
   - What did I love today?
   - What was I good at today?
   - What did the world need from me today?
   - What sustained me today?
3. Watch the compass update as you type
4. Save your reflection to build your journey

## Technology

- Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- No external dependencies
- Uses localStorage for data persistence
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop
